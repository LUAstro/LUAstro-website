Welcome to LUAstro.space!

This is the website for LUAstro! We are a student-run society at Lancaster University, passionate about sharing the wonders of the cosmos. Whether you're an experienced astronomer or just curious about the night sky, our society offers events, workshops, and observing sessions for all levels.

You can stay updated with our latest activities and events through our Instagram and Twitter pages, or (if you're a student at Lancaster University) become a member TODAY!

From sharing the Astrolab that we use in Observing Nights with MPHYS students and researchers, we're closely linked with Lancaster University's Physics Department, including their Observational Astrophysics Research Group in particular. Click the links above to find out more about what they do!
