Welcome to LUAstro.org!

We are a student-run society at Lancaster University, passionate about sharing the wonders of the cosmos. Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or just curious about the night sky, our society offers events, workshops, and observing sessions for all levels.

Stay updated with our latest activities and events through our Instagram and X (formerly Twitter) pages.
